## 📋 Before You Start

### 1. Check Your Network

Make sure your wallet (MetaMask, etc.) is on the correct network:

- ✅ **Ethereum Sepolia** (Chain ID: `11155111`)
- ✅ **Base Sepolia** (Chain ID: `84532`)
- ❌ **NOT Ethereum Mainnet**

### 2. Have Your Wallet Address Ready

Copy your wallet address from MetaMask or your preferred wallet.

### 3. Follow Faucet Instructions

Each faucet may have different requirements:
- Some require account creation (free)
- Some require social media verification
- Some use browser mining

## 🎓 How to Use Faucets

### Step 1: Choose a Faucet
Pick one from the tables above. We recommend starting with:
- **Alchemy Sepolia Faucet** for Ethereum Sepolia
- **Coinbase Base Faucet** for Base Sepolia

### Step 2: Connect Your Wallet
1. Visit the faucet link
2. Connect your wallet when prompted
3. Make sure you're on the correct network

### Step 3: Request Tokens
1. Enter your wallet address (if needed)
2. Complete any verification (if required)
3. Click "Send Me ETH" or similar button
4. Wait for transaction confirmation (1-2 minutes)

### Step 4: Verify You Received ETH
1. Check your wallet balance
2. You should see testnet ETH added
3. If not, try a different faucet

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Faucet says I already claimed today"
**Solution**: Try a different faucet from the list above, or wait 24 hours.

### "Transaction failed"
**Solution**: 
1. Check you're on the right network (Sepolia, not mainnet)
2. Try a different faucet
3. Clear cache and try again

### "Not receiving ETH"
**Solution**:
1. Wait 5 minutes (sometimes it takes time)
2. Check you're looking at the correct network in your wallet
3. Verify the transaction on block explorer (see links below)

### "Faucet requires verification"
**Solution**: 
- Some faucets need social media accounts (Twitter, etc.)
- Try faucets that don't require verification (like PoW faucet)
- Or create the required account (usually free)
